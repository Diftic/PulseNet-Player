Pulse Retro
*Historic • Old Earth • Archive Broadcasts*

Pulse Retro is PulseNet’s time capsule, dedicated to restoring and broadcasting music from Old Earth and early UEE history. It delivers remastered classics alongside carefully preserved recordings, giving listeners access to the sounds that shaped humanity long before it reached the stars.

From analog recordings to early digital archives, Pulse Retro bridges centuries of culture into a single, living broadcast.

This isn’t nostalgia.
It’s continuity.

**Station Tagline:**
*“The past, still playing.”*

## Station Identity

Pulse Retro represents the **foundation of modern music in the ‘Verse**.

It plays:
- Restored Old Earth music across multiple eras
- Early UEE recordings
- Remastered analog and digital archives
- Historically significant tracks and genres

Each piece is carefully curated and enhanced through PulseNet’s **Cultural Preservation Initiative**.

## DJ Profile
**DJ Elias Varn**

**Show**: *Archive Signal*

Elias Varn is a cultural archivist turned broadcaster, specializing in restoring and contextualizing Old Earth media. He approaches each track as a historical artifact, sharing insight into its origin, era, and influence.

He doesn’t just play history.
He preserves it in motion.

**Personality:**
- Calm and deliberate
- Deeply knowledgeable
- Reflective and respectful
- Subtly passionate about preservation

**Signature Intro:**
