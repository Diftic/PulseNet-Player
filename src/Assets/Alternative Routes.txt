Alternative Routes
*Alternative Rock • Indie • Experimental*

Alternative Routes is PulseNet’s station for music that refuses to stay in a lane. From classic alternative rock to indie, post-rock, and genre-blending experimental sounds, it showcases artists who take a different path.

This is where:
- Unfinished ideas become movements
- Quiet songs hit the hardest
- Identity matters more than popularity

Alternative Routes doesn’t chase trends.
It finds what’s next.

**Station Tagline:**
*“Find a different path.”*

# Station Identity

Alternative Routes represents the **independent spirit of the ‘Verse**.

It plays:
- Alternative rock (classic and modern)
- Indie rock and indie pop
- Experimental and fusion genres
- Atmospheric and post-rock

The station thrives on **contrast and discovery**, often introducing artists before they reach wider recognition.

# DJ Profile
DJ Eon Lark

**Show**: Open Frequencies

Eon Lark began as a guitarist in an underground indie band that never broke into the mainstream. After stepping away from performing, he turned to curating music, building a reputation for discovering overlooked talent.

PulseNet brought him in to give those voices a platform.

Personality:
- Thoughtful and observant
- Quietly passionate
- Slightly reserved
- Deeply tuned into emerging sounds

He lets the music speak, and when he talks, it means something.

**Signature Intro:**
*"You’re on Alternative Routes.*
*If you were looking for the usual… you took a wrong turn."*
