HoloStageLive
*Live Concerts • Events • Broadcast Performances*

HoloStage Live is PulseNet’s real-time performance channel, delivering concerts, tours, and special events from across the Empire. From massive arena shows on Terra to intimate sets on remote stations, this is where music is experienced as it happens.

No edits. No filters.
Just the energy of a live crowd and the sound of artists in motion.

**Station Tagline:**
*“LIVE across the ‘Verse.”*

## Station Identity

HoloStage Live represents the **shared experience of music in real time**.

It broadcasts:
- Full live concerts
- Touring artist performances
- Festival stages and special events
- Exclusive PulseNet sessions

The station thrives on energy, **unpredictability, and presence**. Every broadcast is unique.

Audience

HoloStage Live draws in:
- Fans who want to experience concerts remotely
- Citizens unable to attend major events
- Travelers and pilots tuning in mid-flight
- Music enthusiasts across all PulseNet genres
- Anyone who wants to feel part of something bigger

It turns the entire ‘Verse into one audience.

# Host Profile
**DJ/Host Renna Vale**

**Show**: *Live Signal*

Renna Vale is a seasoned live event host known for her ability to carry energy across broadcast channels. She moves between venues, introducing performances and connecting audiences to the moment.

She doesn’t perform.
She amplifies.

Personality:
- Energetic and engaging
- Charismatic and confident
- Quick on her feet
- Thrives in live environments

She knows how to hold the moment without interrupting it.

**Signature Intro:**
*"You’re on HoloStage Live.*
*Wherever you are… you’re in the crowd."*
