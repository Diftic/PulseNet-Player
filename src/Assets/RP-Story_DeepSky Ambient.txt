DeepSky Ambient
*Ambient • Chillwave • Atmospheric*

DeepSky Ambient is PulseNet’s low-intensity, high-immersion station, designed for long-distance travel, focus, and reflection. It delivers slow, evolving soundscapes that blend ambient textures, chillwave elements, and minimalistic compositions.

This is the soundtrack of:
- Deep-space travel between jump points
- Long-haul cargo runs
- Observation decks and research stations
- Quiet hours in the ‘Verse

DeepSky Ambient doesn’t push.
It drifts.

**Station Tagline:**
*“Drift beyond the noise.”*

## Station Identity

DeepSky Ambient represents the **quiet vastness of space**.

It plays:
- Ambient and atmospheric soundscapes
- Chillwave and low-tempo electronic
- Minimal, evolving compositions
- Instrumental and texture-focused audio

The station emphasizes **space, tone, and gradual change**, often with long-form tracks that blend seamlessly together.

## DJ Profile
**DJ Sera Nyx**

**Show**: *Drift Horizon*

Sera Nyx is less a traditional DJ and more a curator of atmosphere. Little is known about her background, but her broadcasts are known for their seamless transitions and carefully layered soundscapes.

She speaks rarely.
When she does, it’s soft and intentional.

Personality:
- Calm and introspective
- Minimal and deliberate
- Deeply attuned to mood and tone
- Almost meditative presence

**Signature Intro:**
*"You’re listening to DeepSky Ambient.*
*Let the noise fall away."*
