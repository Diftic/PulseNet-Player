Solaris Classical
*Classical • Orchestral • Contemporary Symphonic*

Solaris Classical is PulseNet’s refined music station, dedicated to orchestral works spanning Old Earth traditions and modern compositions from across the Empire. It delivers timeless pieces alongside newly written symphonies inspired by life among the stars.

From grand concert halls on Terra to intimate chamber performances aboard orbital stations, Solaris Classical preserves and evolves humanity’s most enduring musical form.

**Station Tagline:**
*“Timeless. Boundless.”*

## Station Identity

Solaris Classical represents the **cultural legacy of humanity**.

It plays:
- Classical compositions from Old Earth archives
- Orchestral and symphonic works from across the UEE
- Modern classical and cinematic scores
- Chamber music and solo performances

The station emphasizes **precision, emotion, and structure**, offering music that rewards attentive listening.

## DJ Profile
**Aria Solenne**

**Show**: *Starlight Symphony*

Aria Solenne is a former conductor who transitioned into broadcasting after years leading orchestras across the core systems. Her deep understanding of composition allows her to guide listeners through each piece with clarity and insight.

She doesn’t just present music.
She interprets it.

Personality:
- Poised and articulate
- Calm and expressive
- Deeply knowledgeable
- Emotionally intuitive

Her presence adds meaning without ever overpowering the music.

**Signature Intro:**
*"You’re listening to Solaris Classical.*
*Let the music speak."*
