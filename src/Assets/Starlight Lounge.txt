Starlight Lounge
*Jazz • Lounge • Slowwave*

Starlight Lounge is PulseNet’s late-night sanctuary, delivering smooth jazz, lounge, and downtempo soundscapes across the Empire. It’s designed for unwinding, conversation, and those quiet hours between destinations.

From classic jazz influences to modern lounge fusion and atmospheric slowwave, the station creates a warm, refined atmosphere wherever it’s played.

**Station Tagline:**
*“Ease into the night.”*

## Station Identity

Starlight Lounge represents the quiet elegance of the ‘Verse.

It plays:
- Jazz (classic and contemporary)
- Lounge and chill fusion
- Downtempo and slowwave
- Instrumental and soft vocal performances

The station emphasizes mood, space, and subtlety, allowing the music to blend seamlessly into the environment.

## DJ Profile
**DJ Liora Venn**

**Show**: Velvet Orbit

Liora Venn built her career performing in small jazz venues before transitioning into broadcasting. Known for her smooth voice and impeccable timing, she curates each set like a live performance.

She doesn’t interrupt the mood.
She shapes it.

Personality:
- Warm and inviting
- Soft-spoken and refined
- Attentive to atmosphere
- Effortlessly composed

Her presence feels like part of the music itself.

**Signature Intro:**
*"You’re listening to Starlight Lounge.*
*Let the night settle in."*
