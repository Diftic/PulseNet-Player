IronChord
*Rock • Industrial • Metal*

IronChord is PulseNet’s heavy music station, built for the workers, fighters, and crews who keep the ‘Verse running. From hard rock and industrial to full-throttle metal, this station delivers relentless sound with no compromise.

This is music forged in:
- Shipyards and drydocks
- Mining rigs and salvage fields
- Security patrol routes
- Industrial districts and frontier outposts

IronChord doesn’t soften the edges.
It sharpens them.

**Station Tagline:**
*“Forged in Sound.”*

## Station Identity

IronChord represents the **industrial backbone of the Empire**.

It plays:
- Hard rock and heavy metal
- Industrial and mechanical-driven music
- High-energy, aggressive tracks
- Live performances recorded in working environments

The station emphasizes **power, rhythm, and grit**, often blending mechanical soundscapes with music.

## DJ Profile
### Viktor Halden

**Show**: *Metal in the Void*

Viktor Halden operates out of a mobile broadcast rig built into a retrofitted mining ship. He travels between shipyards and industrial zones, recording live performances and bringing raw, unfiltered sound directly to listeners.

He doesn’t just play music.
He captures it where it lives.

**Personality**:
- Intense and direct
- No-nonsense delivery
- Deeply connected to industrial culture
- Respects authenticity above all

Signature Intro:
*"You’re on IronChord.*
*Turn it up. The void doesn’t mind."*
