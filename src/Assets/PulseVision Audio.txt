PulseVision Audio
*Soundtracks • Cinematic • Visual Media Audio*

PulseVision Audio is the audio arm of PulseNet’s visual network, delivering music and sound drawn from mobiVid productions, documentaries, live broadcasts, and cinematic storytelling across the ‘Verse.

From sweeping orchestral scores to ambient scene design and high-impact action tracks, this station brings the emotional core of visual media into pure audio form.

You don’t need the screen.
You already feel it.

**Station Tagline:**
*“Hear the story.”*

## Station Identity

PulseVision Audio represents the **sound of storytelling**.

It plays:
- Film and mobiVid soundtracks
- Documentary scores
- Cinematic orchestral and hybrid compositions
- Ambient and scene-based audio design

The station emphasizes **emotion, pacing, and narrative**, often building entire segments like a story arc.

## DJ Profile
**DJ Ren Blackfeather**

**Show**: *Frame by Frame*

Ren Blackfeather is a former sound designer who transitioned into broadcasting, bringing a deep understanding of how audio shapes narrative. He curates each segment like a scene, guiding listeners through emotional shifts without needing visuals.

He doesn’t just play tracks.
He builds sequences.

Personality:
- Calm and deliberate
- Highly creative
- Detail-oriented
- Emotionally intuitive

He speaks sparingly, letting the sound carry the weight.

**Signature Intro:**
*"You’re on PulseVision Audio.*
*Close your eyes. It’s all there."*
