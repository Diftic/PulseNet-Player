Frontline Frequency
*Military • Ceremonial • Patriotic*

Frontline Frequency is PulseNet’s military and ceremonial station, dedicated to the traditions, history, and spirit of the United Empire of Earth’s armed forces. It features military bands, orchestral marches, and commemorative compositions alongside modern pieces inspired by service across the ‘Verse.

From fleet formations to remembrance ceremonies, this station reflects discipline, unity, and sacrifice.

It doesn’t glorify conflict.
It remembers the people who stand in it.

**Station Tagline:**
*“Honor in every signal.”*

## Station Identity

Frontline Frequency represents the **discipline and legacy of service**.

It plays:
- Military band performances and marches
- Orchestral and ceremonial compositions
- Naval and UEE-inspired music
- Commemorative and memorial pieces

The station emphasizes **structure, respect, and tradition**, with programming that reflects both pride and remembrance.

## DJ Profile
**Commander Elias Rourke** (Ret.)

**Show**: *Signal of Service*

Elias Rourke served decades in the UEE Navy before retiring and transitioning into broadcasting. His experience gives him a deep understanding of the music’s meaning, and he approaches every broadcast with respect and clarity.

He doesn’t dramatize.
He reflects.

Personality:
- Composed and authoritative
- Respectful and measured
- Thoughtful and grounded
- Deeply connected to service culture

**Signature Intro:**
"This is Frontline Frequency.
For those who serve, and those who remember."
