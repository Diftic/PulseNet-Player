EchoVerse
*Indie • Emerging Artists • Citizen Creator Network*

EchoVerse is PulseNet’s discovery station, dedicated entirely to independent artists and creators across the Empire. It exists as a platform for new voices, experimental sounds, and music that hasn’t yet reached the mainstream.

Every track on EchoVerse started somewhere small:
- A cargo bay recording setup
- A home studio in a megacity tower
- A handheld recorder on a frontier world

EchoVerse gives those signals a way to travel.

**Station Tagline:**
*“New voices. New signals.”*

## Station Identity

EchoVerse represents the **future of sound in the ‘Verse**.

It plays:
- Indie music across all genres
- First-release tracks and demos
- Experimental and genre-blending work
- Submissions from PulseNet’s **Citizen Creator Program**

The station is constantly evolving, with new artists appearing daily.

# DJ Profile
**DJ Juno Mirai**

**Show**: *New Signals*

Juno Mirai is PulseNet’s lead curator for independent music. With a background in artist development and digital media, she specializes in finding talent before it reaches wider recognition.

She listens to everything.

Juno approaches each broadcast like a showcase, carefully selecting tracks that deserve to be heard and giving context to the artists behind them.

**Personality:**
- Insightful and encouraging
- Passionate about creativity
- Supportive of new artists
- Curious and forward-thinking

She treats every creator like they matter… because they do.

**Signature Intro:**
*"You’re listening to EchoVerse.*
*Let’s hear what’s next."*
