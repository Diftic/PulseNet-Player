The Cargo Deck
Spacer Talk • Mixed Music • Hauler Culture

The Cargo Deck is PulseNet’s working-class broadcast for haulers, traders, and anyone moving goods across the ‘Verse. It blends music with live talk segments, pilot check-ins, and real-time transmissions from the spacelanes.

This is where:
- Routes are discussed
- Stories are shared
- Requests are played live
- And no one flies completely alone

It’s part station, part lifeline.

**Station Tagline:**
*“You’re not flying solo.”*

## Station Identity

The Cargo Deck  represents the **voice of the spacelanes**.

It plays:
- A rotating mix of genres (country, rock, indie, light electronic)
- Listener-requested tracks
- Talk segments and live call-ins
- Field recordings from pilots across systems

The station thrives on **community and connection**, not just music.

It’s often heard in:

## DJ Profile
**Luca Bardan**

**Show**: *Midnight Freight*

Luca Bardan is the voice most spacers recognize. A former trade pilot turned broadcaster, he built his reputation by staying on the line longer than anyone else, taking calls from across the Empire and turning them into shared stories.

He keeps the signal open.

**Personality:**
- Friendly and conversational
- Grounded and relatable
- Quick to respond and engage
- Genuinely invested in the community

He talks like someone who’s lived the routes… because he has.

**Signature Intro:**
*"This is Luca Verdan on The Cargo Deck.*
*If you’re out there hauling tonight… check in."*
