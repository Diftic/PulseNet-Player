Spectrum Beats

*Trending Hits • Chart-Toppers • Empire Pop*

Spectrum Beats is PulseNet’s flagship station and its **largest audience channel**, delivering the most popular music across the United Empire of Earth in real time. It tracks what the ‘Verse is listening to right now and turns it into a constant, high-energy broadcast.

From polished pop anthems to crossover hits from every genre, Spectrum Beats is where music becomes shared culture across systems.

If it’s everywhere… it’s here.

## Station Identity

Spectrum Beats represents the unified sound of the Empire.

It plays:
- Chart-topping hits across all genres
- Crossover tracks (electronic, hip-hop, rock, fusion)
- Viral and trending releases
- Major artist debuts

The station updates constantly using PulseNet’s **PulseSync data network**, pulling listening trends from across systems to keep the playlist fresh.

Station Tagline:
*“Trending across the ‘Verse.”*

## DJ Profile
**DJ Cassian Vire**

**Show**: *Empire Topline*

Cassian Vire is the voice of what’s trending. Charismatic, polished, and always ahead of the curve, he delivers the latest hits with confidence and energy.

Before PulseNet, Cassian worked as a media personality covering entertainment trends across Terra. His ability to predict breakout tracks made him a natural fit for Spectrum Beats.

**Personality:**
- Energetic and engaging
- Smooth and professional
- Trend-savvy
- Fast-paced delivery

He thrives on momentum and keeps the station moving.

**Signature Intro**:
