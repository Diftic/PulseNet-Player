NovaBeat
*Electronic • Synthwave • Club Music*

NovaBeat is PulseNet’s high-energy electronic station, broadcasting the sound of nightlife across the Empire. From synthwave and house to high-tempo club tracks and experimental electronic fusion, NovaBeat fuels the pulse of modern civilization.

It dominates the soundscape of Terra’s nightlife, ArcCorp’s megacities, and every station where lights stay on long after standard cycle.

This is where the night begins.

Station Tagline:
*“Feel the Pulse.”*

## Station Identity
NovaBeat represents the energy of the core systems.

It plays:
- Electronic (house, techno, synthwave)
- Club and dance music
- High-energy remixes and live DJ sets
- Experimental electronic fusion

The station thrives on **momentum and atmosphere**, creating a continuous flow designed for movement, energy, and immersion.

## DJ Profile
**DJ Orion Vale**

**Show**: *Neon Horizon*

Orion Vale is one of the most recognizable electronic DJs in the Empire. Broadcasting from Terra’s upper districts, his show blends cutting-edge tracks with seamless transitions that keep listeners locked in for hours.

He’s known for debuting tracks before they hit Spectrum Beats and for hosting live broadcasts from zero-gravity clubs.

**Personality:**
- Energetic and charismatic
- Smooth and immersive delivery
- Trend-setting and forward-thinking
- Thrives in live environments

He doesn’t just play music; he builds a continuous experience.

**Signature Intro**:
*"You’re on NovaBeat.*
*Let the night take over."*
